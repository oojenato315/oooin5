- We will reapply the task management method according to the Scrum model with a 2-week Sprint

  - At the beginning of each sprint, the team will organize a meeting to determine:
    - the status of the previous sprint
    - tasks/stories to be completed in this sprint

  - Each individual must choose their task/story in that sprint based on the backlog [All stories]
    - At the end of the first day of the sprint, each member must complete their story/task and estimate for each task
    - Each task must not be estimated for more than 24 hours.
    - Must estimate according to the principle: buffer 20%-30% for each task. to save time for possible tasks: being blocked, needing support for other teams
    - Important point: the tasks that have been included in the sprint must be completed, unless they are blocked and cannot be resolved (will be through daily report for the whole team to understand and have a solution)
  - Daily status update through 1 Daily report channel, with content:
    - Tasks in progress, task progress
    - Difficulties (if any) and solutions
  - The release needs to be done by the leaders:
    - Pipeline: Phuc
    - BE: Nhat
    - FE: Marsel

- Create stories in the backlog [All stories]
  - This is the main task of the Manager, but everyone can create stories in the backlog
  - Each story needs to have enough information according to the template: [Template] User story - Reset password
  - The decision to include stories in the sprint will be decided by the team at the beginning of the sprint
